PGCoE Response to Measles

In response to the growing measles outbreak in the United States and beyond, the PGCoE has brought together protocols for processing measles for public health investigations and surveillance developed by PGCoE partners. These wet laboratory protocols are established workflows in either public health or academic laboratories, and can be foundational resource for establishing in-house measles sequencing.

Additionally, CDC and state dashboard links are provided for ease of access for those looking to gain situational awareness of the current measle virus case loads/outbreak.
